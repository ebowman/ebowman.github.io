Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a breathing session?

Open FlöBreath on your watch, select a breathing pattern, and tap Start. Follow the haptic and visual cues to guide your breathing.

What breathing patterns are available?

FlöBreath includes several patterns: Box Breathing (4-4-4-4), 4-7-8 Relaxation, Coherence Breathing, Physiological Sigh, Triangle Breathing, Extended Exhale, and 2-1 Breathing. Each has a different rhythm suited to different goals like relaxation, focus, or sleep.

Why does FlöBreath need Health access?

FlöBreath reads your heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV) during sessions to show you how your body responds to breathing exercises. All data stays on your device.

Where is my data stored?

All session data is stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch. Nothing is sent to any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete a session?

In the session history on the iOS app, swipe left on any session to delete it.

Can I use FlöBreath without granting Health access?

The breathing guide works without Health access, but you won't see heart rate or HRV feedback for your sessions.
